Privacy declaration Auhhenticator for Windows

General

This is the privacy declaration of Authenticator for Windows. With all respect for your privacy, we try to limit the required data to a minimal amount. Dit is de privacyverklaring van Authenticator for Windows. Met alle respect voor uw privacy proberen we de benodigde gegevens te beperken tot een minimum. We always adhere to the rules set by the Data Protection Act. For all permissions the app uses, we explain why it's being used.

This statement may be amended unilaterally at any time.

Camera

The camera in your device can be used to scan codes. During the scanning there's being searched to a QR code to read. These pictures are always locally and will NEVER be sent over the internet.

Internet connection

Your internet connection is being used to retrieve the most precise time from an NTP server. You can turn this off at any time to prevent data usage.
